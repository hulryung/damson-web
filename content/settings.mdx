# Settings

Damson has a full settings UI. Themes hot-reload (including light/dark switches),
so you can tweak and see the result immediately. Here's what you can configure.

## Text & font

- **Font family and size**
- **Ligatures** — programming ligatures on or off
- **Double-width icons** — how wide certain symbol glyphs render
- **Custom word separators** — control what double-click selects

## Theme & appearance

- **Theme** with light/dark hot-reload
- **Background opacity and blur** — translucent windows over your desktop
- **Padding** — horizontal and vertical insets around the grid
- **Transparent tab bar**
- **Scrollbar visibility**
- **Screen effects** — optional CRT-style effect with adjustable intensity
- **Glyph appear / disappear animations**

## Cursor

- **Cursor shape** (block and others)
- **Cursor blink** on or off

## Panes & tabs

- **Tab bar style** and **tab transition style** (slide / spring settle)
- **Active-pane indicator** — e.g. dim the inactive panes
- **Focus follows mouse**
- **Animations** — global on/off
- **New-tab directory** — where new tabs and panes start

## Scrollback & input

- **Scrollback lines** (default 10,000)
- **Restore scrollback** on relaunch
- **Scroll speed**
- **Copy on select**
- **Press and hold** behavior

## Updates

- **Automatic updates** (Sparkle) on or off
- **Check for Updates** on demand, under **Advanced**

---

Most settings take effect immediately. For the keyboard shortcuts behind tabs,
panes, and layouts, see [Getting Started](/getting-started) — and remember every
shortcut is remappable.
